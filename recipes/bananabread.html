Banana bread

Ingredients list

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 21/3 cups of smashed overripe bananas

Steps to follow

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar
  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended
  5. Stir banan mixture into flour mixtures; stir just to moisten
  6. Pour batter into prepared loaf

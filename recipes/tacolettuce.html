Ingredients list

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen corn
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • romaine leaves, rinsed and dried

Steps to follow

  1. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Cook and stir ground turkey in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Stir in corn, black beans, diced tomatoes, and taco seasoning
  4. Bring to a simmer and cook until heated through, 10 to 20 minutes
